about me

Hi, I’m Max van Helvert, a teacher based in Wodonga with a growing passion for web design. Over the past year, I’ve been developing my skills in creating clean, functional, and easy-to-manage websites.

I started Max Web Design to help local businesses and community groups establish a professional online presence without the high costs or confusion that often come with it. My goal is to provide straightforward, affordable websites that look great and work seamlessly — so you can focus on running your business.

Whether you need a new website, a fresh redesign, or some simple updates, I’d love to help bring your project to life.
